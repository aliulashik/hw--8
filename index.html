Tiger

Tigers live in diverse habitats, from tropical forests to grasslands. They are apex predators, feeding mainly on deer, wild boar, and other mammals. Unlike most cats, tigers enjoy water and are excellent swimmers. Sadly, they face threats from habitat loss and poaching, putting their survival at great risk today.

Horse

The horse is a strong and graceful animal, valued by humans for transport, farming, and companionship. Known for speed and endurance, horses have played vital roles in history and culture.
